About Us

Who we are

Born in Malaysia in 2015, Nasty Juice has grown from a small, fearless e-liquid maker into a global lifestyle brand. Today, we’re more than just a name in vaping—we’re a worldwide movement, recognized in over 80 countries across six continents. From our very first blends, we’ve pushed the limits of flavor, creativity, and culture. Our mission is simple: to deliver bold, unforgettable taste experiences that stand out in a crowded world.

What we stand for

• Innovation at the Core – Every flavor is crafted at Nasty LABS, our R&D hub where science meets creativity. • Quality You Can Trust – All of our products are TPD-compliant, independently tested, and built to meet the highest safety standards. • Global Recognition – With awards for Best Branding, Best Fruit Flavor, Best Salt Nicotine, and Best Disposable, Nasty Juice is setting benchmarks in the industry. • Culture & Community – Nasty isn’t just about vaping—it’s about expressing yourself. Our street-inspired designs and vibrant packaging reflect the bold personalities of our fans.

Our Journey

• 2015 – Founded in Tampin, Malaysia. • 2016 – Expanded to the UK & Europe with signature flavors like Cush Man, Bad Blood, and Slow Blow. • 2017–2020 – Became a global brand, winning major international awards and building presence across the world. • Today – A worldwide network with offices in Malaysia, Indonesia, India, USA, UK, UAE, and South Korea.

Our Vision

By 2030, we aim to be the world’s most recognized ENDS (Electronic Nicotine Delivery System) provider, leading with innovation, design, and culture.

The Nasy Experience

Every bottle tells a story. Every flavor is crafted to ignite your senses. Whether you’re exploring classics like Cush Man Mango or diving into new blends, Nasty Juice is here to make vaping bold, fresh, and unforgettable. Welcome to the Nasty family. This is more than a brand—this is a lifestyle.
